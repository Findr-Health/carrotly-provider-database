# Findr Health Provider Participation Agreement
## Plain-Language Summary & Recommendations

---

## Document Overview

The Provider Participation Agreement is the contract between Findr Health and healthcare providers who wish to list their services on the platform. It establishes the legal relationship, responsibilities, and protections for both parties.

---

## Plain-Language Summary of Key Terms

### 1. Independent Contractor Status (Article 2)
**What it means:** You are NOT an employee of Findr Health. You're an independent business owner who uses our platform. This means:
- You control how you deliver services
- You're responsible for your own taxes (we send you a 1099)
- You don't get employee benefits from us
- You can't make commitments on behalf of Findr Health

### 2. Platform Access & Listings (Article 3)
**What it means:** When you join, you can:
- Create a profile listing your services
- Set your own prices
- Accept bookings from patients

**Your responsibilities:**
- Keep your information accurate and up-to-date
- Don't post false or misleading information
- Keep your login credentials secure

### 3. Fees & Payment (Article 4)
**What it means:** Here's how you get paid:
- Patients pay through the platform
- We deduct **15% platform fee** from each booking
- Stripe deducts approximately **2.9% + $0.30** for payment processing
- **You receive approximately 82%** of what the patient pays
- Payouts go to your connected Stripe account (daily, weekly, or monthly - your choice)

**Example:** Patient pays $100 → You receive ~$82

### 4. Cancellations & Refunds (Article 5)
**What it means:** You choose either Standard or Moderate cancellation policy:

| Policy | Free Cancellation | 25% Fee | 50% Fee | No-Show |
|--------|-------------------|---------|---------|---------|
| **Standard** | 24+ hours | 12-24 hrs | <12 hrs | 100% |
| **Moderate** | 48+ hours | 24-48 hrs | <24 hrs | 100% |

**Important rules:**
- If YOU cancel, patient always gets 100% refund
- You CAN waive fees for patients on a case-by-case basis
- Findr Health may issue refunds in certain situations (no-shows, safety concerns, etc.)

### 5. Licensing & Credentials (Article 6)
**What it means:** You must:
- Hold all required professional licenses for your services
- Keep those licenses current and in good standing
- Tell us immediately if your license status changes
- Only offer services within your scope of practice

**Note:** We rely on your self-attestation - you're certifying everything is accurate.

### 6. Insurance Requirements (Article 7)
**What it means:** You must carry professional liability (malpractice) insurance with:
- **$1,000,000** minimum per occurrence
- **$3,000,000** minimum annual aggregate

We may ask for proof, and you must tell us if your coverage lapses.

### 7. HIPAA & Data Protection (Article 8)
**What it means:**
- **You** are responsible for your own HIPAA compliance
- Our platform handles scheduling and payments, NOT medical records
- **Don't send Protected Health Information (PHI)** through our messaging unless we have a Business Associate Agreement
- If you need to share PHI through the platform, contact us first

### 8. Conduct Standards (Article 9)
**What it means:** You agree to:
- Act professionally and follow all applicable laws
- Not discriminate against patients
- Follow email/text marketing laws (CAN-SPAM, TCPA)
- Not circumvent our platform to avoid fees
- Not engage in fraud or deceptive practices

### 9. Reviews & Ratings (Article 10)
**What it means:**
- Patients can leave reviews
- You can't buy, fake, or manipulate reviews
- You can't threaten patients over negative reviews
- You can respond professionally to reviews (don't share patient info!)

### 10. Intellectual Property (Article 11)
**What it means:**
- Findr Health owns the platform and our brand
- You own your content, but give us permission to display it on the platform
- Don't use our logo without permission

### 11. Indemnification (Article 12)
**What it means:** If someone sues Findr Health because of something YOU did (malpractice, breach of contract, HIPAA violation, etc.), **you agree to cover our legal costs and any damages.**

### 12. Limitation of Liability (Article 13)
**What it means:**
- The platform is provided "as is" - we don't guarantee it's perfect
- We're not liable for indirect damages (lost profits, etc.)
- Our maximum liability to you is capped at the platform fees we collected from you in the past 12 months

### 13. Term & Termination (Article 14)
**What it means:**
- Either party can end this with **30 days notice**
- We can terminate **immediately** if you violate the agreement, lose your license, or commit fraud
- If you leave, you must fulfill existing bookings or refund patients

### 14. Dispute Resolution (Article 15)
**What it means:**
- Try to work it out informally first (30 days)
- If that fails, disputes go to **binding arbitration** (not court)
- Arbitration happens in Bozeman, Montana under AAA rules
- **No class action lawsuits** - disputes are individual only
- You have **30 days to opt out** of arbitration after signing

### 15. Confidentiality (Article 16)
**What it means:** Don't share Findr Health's confidential business information, and we won't share yours.

### 16. General Terms (Article 17)
**What it means:**
- **Montana law** governs this agreement
- We can update terms with 30 days notice
- If one part is invalid, the rest still applies
- You can't transfer this agreement to someone else

### 17. Data Use Consent (Article 18)
**What it means:** By using the platform, you consent to us collecting and using your business data for platform operations, analytics, and legal compliance. We may share data with service providers like Stripe.
