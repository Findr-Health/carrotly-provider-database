# Pilot KPI Dashboard (Starter)

**Run Name:** 2025-09-24 Fresh Financial Model Rebuild — Pilot-First
**Pilot Geography:** Bozeman, MT
**Pilot Window:** 2025-10 → 2026-03

## KPIs (Targets)
- Provider activation rate ≥ 50% live ≤ 14 days; retention ≥ 60% post fee-holiday
- Bookings per provider (median) ≥ 8 / mo; top quartile ≥ 12 / mo
- First→repeat conversion (60d) ≥ 35%
- Carrot burn/GMV ≤ 6% in M1, trending ≤ 3% by M3
- Blended CAC-u ≤ $10 by M3
- NPS (user/provider) ≥ 45 / ≥ 40

## Red Flags (Trigger corrective levers)
- Carrot burn/GMV > 8% for 2 consecutive months
- Provider churn > 20% post fee-holiday
- Booking growth < 10% MoM for 2 months

## Corrective Levers (examples)
- Shift from platform-funded carrots → provider-funded boosts
- Narrow category focus to high AOV & high repeat
- Increase employer/payer channel share; reduce paid ads
- Introduce stepwise take rate for top-performing providers post-pilot
