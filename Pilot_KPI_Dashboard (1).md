# Pilot KPI Dashboard — WellNow UC (Chicago, IL)

**Run Name:** 2025-09-24 FinModel — WellNow UC Micro-Pilot (Chicago IL)
**Pilot Window:** 2025-10 → 2025-12

## Volume Targets (90 days)
- Total completed transactions: **400–700** (stretch: 900)
- Repeat share by Month 3: **≥40%** of monthly bookings
- Bookings growth: **≥25% MoM**

## Incentive Budget
- Total carrots budget: **$10,000** (hard cap)
- Default payout: **$10** first booking; **$5** repeat

## Quality & Retention
- First→repeat within 60 days: **≥35%**
- Refund/chargebacks: **≤2% of GMV**

## Red Flags & Levers
- If total bookings < 300 by Month 2 → raise referral bonus by $2, add SMS reactivation, geo-concentrate to 4–6 highest-volume sites
- If repeat < 30% by Month 3 → launch “5th visit $15” (provider co-funded) + post-visit reminder cadence (48h, 14d, 45d)
- If carrots budget depletes early → switch to provider-funded boosts and reduce first-booking bonus to $7
