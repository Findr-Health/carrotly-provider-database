## 3. App Description

### Short Description (Promotional Text - 170 chars)
```
Find healthcare providers near you with transparent, upfront pricing. Compare costs, read reviews, and book appointments instantly. Your health, your choice.
```

### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
Findr Health makes finding and booking healthcare simple and transparent.

FIND PROVIDERS NEAR YOU
Search thousands of healthcare providers including doctors, dentists, therapists, nutritionists, skincare specialists, and wellness professionals. Filter by location, specialty, price range, and ratings to find the perfect match.

SEE PRICES UPFRONT
No more surprise bills. Every provider on Findr Health displays their cash-pay prices before you book. Compare costs across providers and make informed decisions about your care.

BOOK INSTANTLY
Skip the phone calls and waiting. Book appointments directly through the app with real-time availability. Choose your preferred date, time, and provider in just a few taps.

MEET CLARITY - YOUR HEALTH ASSISTANT
Have questions about what type of provider you need? Our AI assistant Clarity helps you navigate healthcare options, compare services, and find the right care for your needs.

MANAGE YOUR APPOINTMENTS
View upcoming appointments, reschedule when needed, and keep track of your healthcare history all in one place. Get reminders so you never miss an appointment.

TRUSTED PROVIDERS
Every provider on Findr Health is verified and reviewed by real patients. Read authentic reviews and ratings to choose with confidence.

KEY FEATURES:
• Search by specialty, location, and price
• View transparent cash-pay pricing
• Book appointments instantly
• AI-powered health assistant
• Manage appointments and history
• Read verified patient reviews
• Save favorite providers
• Get appointment reminders

Whether you need a routine checkup, dental cleaning, therapy session, or specialized treatment, Findr Health helps you find quality care at prices you can understand.

Download now and take control of your healthcare journey.
```
